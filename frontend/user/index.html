The Light We Carry: Our Journey

Shelter of Light is but an independent rescue that I run generally alone. We face a lot of challenges from judgment, to minimal finances, to needing to figure things out on my own, to balancing everything at the same time. My heart has also been in shatters lately due to the losses and trials of this year, but this anniversary, I want to look into the good things. Into the small moments that remind us that it is all still very much worth it. Things will get better, hopefully, but right now, these glimmers of hope are important, as well.
